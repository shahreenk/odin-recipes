Turkey Chili

Description

This hearty turkey chili recipe, made with easy-to-find ingredients and pantry staples, will warm you up from the inside out.

Ingredients

  • One pound ground turkey
  • One onion
  • Tablespoon of fresh garlic
  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Canned Kidney beans
  • Chili powder
  • Paprika
  • Dried oregano
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the turkey in olive oil. Add the onion and cook until tender.
  2. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes.
